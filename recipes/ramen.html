A Bowl of Ramen

Dish Details

  • 600 Calories
  • 30g of Fat
  • 20g of Protein

Preparation Details

  • Estimated Time: 1 Hour and 15 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Onion, Chopped
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Inch Piece of Ginger, Peeled and Minced
  • 2 Tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Mirin
  • 4 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 1 Package Ramen Noodles
  • 1/2 Cup Carrots, Shredded
  • 1/2 Cup Cabbage, Chopped
  • 1/4 Cup Green Onions, Chopped
  • 1 Hard-Boiled Egg, Sliced
  • Sesame Seeds For Garnish

Step-By-Step Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the pork belly, onion, carrots, celery, ginger, and garlic. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the pork is browned and the vegetables are softened.
  2. Add the chicken broth, soy sauce, mirin, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and black peppper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour, or until the pork is tender.
  3. While the broth is simmering, cook the ramen noodles according to the package directions.
  4. To assemble the ramen, divide the noodles between bowls. Ladle the broth over the noodles and top with the pork, egg, nori, menma, and scallions. Serve immediately.
